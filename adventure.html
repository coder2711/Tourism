Adventure Tourism

In the last couple of years Adventure Sports have gained a lot of popularity in the Indian market. Tourists coming to India are enthralled and captivated by the divergent topography present here. The mountains, rivers, oceans, deserts help provide both soft and extreme adventures. The outdoor activities help in de-stressing, rejuvenating and adding an element of thrill to the adventurer. It is seen as a great way to travel and explore far off places and experience a never before sense of achievment and accomplishment.

The different Adventure Sports available in India are Skiing, Trekking, Mountain Climbing, River Rafting, Paragliding, Sky Diving, Motorcycle Touring, Caving, Hot Air Ballooning, Scuba Diving, Parasailing, Microlite Flying etc.

The destinations facilitating Adventure Tourism are Rishikesh, Shimla, Meghalaya, Goa, Mysuru, Bir Billing, Gulmarg, Jaipur, Bengaluru and many more.
