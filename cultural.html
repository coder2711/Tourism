Cultural Tourism

The Indian Civilization is at least 8000 years old. It is the birth place of many religions which include Hinduism, Jainism, Buddhism and Sikhism. India has also been ruled by different Indian dynasties, Mughals, English, Portuguese etc. As a result there is a great diversity in the countries culture. The different traditions and rituals, the various art forms, temples, monuments etc, all reflect the varied Indian cultural heritage.

There are a plethora of tourist attractions covering the length and breadth of the country. Some of the mesmerizing spots are the Vivekanand Rock Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue in Kanyakumari, Meenakshi Temple and Tirumalai Nayak Palace in Madurai, the Golconda Fort and Charminar in Hyderabad, the Konark Sun Temple in Puri, the Mysore Palace of Mysuru, the Aguada Fort and
Se Cathedral in Goa, Ajanta and Ellora Caves in Aurangabad, Tawang monastery in Arunachal Pradesh and many more.

A few of the best places to visit and experience the cultural fervour and traditions are Varanasi, Prayagraj, Mathura, Kolkata, Mumbai, Panji, Mysuru etc.
