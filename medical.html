Medical Tourism

Medical Tourism is a fast growing sector in India. The outstanding calibre of the doctors, nurses and the entire medical fraternity along with the availability of best treatment at lower costs has made India a popular destination for medical tourists.
Some of the other factors encouraging patients to take treatment in India are ease of travel including formalities regarding obtaining the tourist visa, minimum waiting period for getting treatment and lower cost of recuperating after the treatment is done.

Most of the medical tourists who come to India are from the Middle East, South East Asia, Africa and SAARC region. We also have patients coming from US, UK, Canada and Australia.

Some of the popular medical tourism destinations in India are Chennai, Mumbai, New Dehli, Bengaluru, Coimbatore, Hyderabad.
Few of the hospitals providing the service are Apollo Hospitals, Fortis Hospitals, Global Hospitals, Sri Ramachandra Medical Centre, Rockland Hospitals and Columbia Asia Referral Hospital.
